Global Doctor Migration
Understanding the flow and economic impact of healthcare professionals worldwide

About our Main Visualisation

  • We aim to illustrate the annual flow of doctors in OECD countries using a Choropleth map.
  • The migration rate spans from 2010 to 2022, allowing viewers to navigate using a slide bar.
  • Hover functionality is provided, enabling viewers to view the name of the country and its exact migration number for the specific year.
  • Additionally, hovering over any country triggers the display of a line chart and scatter plot chart, both representing the migration rate in the respective country from 2010 to 2022. This enables viewers to gain a better understanding of the fluctuations in each country.
  • This visualisation has been developed using data from the OECD World Health Statistics Database.
  • An option to toggle to color-blind view is also provided.
  • Click on the image on right to further explore it

Our GDP Visualisation

  • We aim to let viewers compare how GDP affects and gets affected by doctor migrations. The color scale is the same as used in the heatmap to make the comparison easier.
  • The GDP ranges from 2010 to 2022, allowing viewers to navigate using a slide bar, and the countries in the treemap are the same as in our heatmap.
  • The bigger the box, the higher the GDP of the country. Box sizes will keep on changing as moving the year slider.
  • Hover functionality is provided, enabling viewers to view the name of the country and its exact GDP per capita in dollars for the specific year.
  • This visualisation has been developed using data from the database.
  • Click on the image on the left to further explore it.
